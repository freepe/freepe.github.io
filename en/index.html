About FreePe project

Public organization FreePe Foundation implements the project FreePe to satisfy the interests and protect the rights and freedoms of the planet Earth population. General idea is to create decentralized socio-economic network for effective collaboration and co-creation on Internet and beyond. Main goal is to unite people around the world and provide freedom of exchange of services, information and resources, based on the mutual control of network participants, as well as the usage of a sustainable value equivalent.

FreePenny proposal

Cryptoactive FreePenny is developed for all kinds of economic interactions in FreePe. The cost of 1 FPN is always equal to the average minimum cost of 1 hour work of the participant. FreePenny generation is based on the performing of useful work or provision of resources.Thus, each FPN is confirmed by real value and it can always be used to pay for goods and services or for accumulative purposes.The quality of participant's life is steadily improving, due to increasing of opportunities, which promotes the rising of FreePenny cost.

  • Expanding the range of activities and provided services.
  • Manufacture of food, clothing, electronics and useful goods.
  • Synthesis of new knowledge, technologies, practical solutions.
