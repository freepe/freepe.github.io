About FreePe project

Public organization FreePe Foundation implements the project FreePe to satisfy the interests and protect the rights and freedoms of the planet Earth population. Main idea is to create decentralized socio-economic network for effective collaboration and co-creation on Internet and beyond. Main goal is to provide people with quantum space of possibilities - the general spectre of what human or organization could perform using talants, free resources and available knowledge.
