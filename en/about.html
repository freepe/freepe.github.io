FreePe mission

FreePe is a project to create a prosperous association of Free People in a Free World. International non-profit public organization "FreePe Foundation" has been establised in order to implement, support, lelalize and curate the project. Objectives of organization are: satisfy and protect the social, economic, ecological, cultural, creative and other rights or interests of individuals, enterprises, financial, academic institutions and government authorities; contribute to improving the economic, social and environmental climate in Ukraine and the world. Main activities of FreePe Foundation are:

  • Create conditions for the free flow of information, goods and services in the Internet using alternative payment instruments by creating a decentralized socio-economic network FreePe.
  • Provide all possible support, develop FreePe project and all its components.
  • Create conditions so that every member of the organization feel as full participant and know that his success is the success of the entire organization.
  • Promote the unification of citizens' efforts to create a successful and prosperous society.
  • Participate in the implementation of international, national, regional, local and own programs.
  • Collaborate with different public and other institutions, organizations, enterprises.
  • Faciliate the participation of the organization members in international events, seminars that are relevant to statutory activities of the organization.
  • Contribute to organization of non-profit educational events, congresses, conferences, thematic improvements, lectures, seminars, round tables, press conferences, forums, meetings, public hearings, workshops, conventions, socio-cultural and other public events that focus on the needs and interests of the members organization with the participation of domestic and international experts.
  • Promoting a healthy way of life, correct nutrition, family and human values.
  • Founding of the mass media in order to achieve the statutory purposes and facilitate the dissemination of information on issues related to the organization's goals.
  • Facilitate training and education for members of the organization and other people.
  • Developing of a full-fledged infrastructure to meet the social, economic, scientific, household and other needs and interests of organization members.

The operating principle of FreePe

Participants of FreePe network set goals that are necessary for prosperity, which are broken down into tasks. For example, today's goal is to develop decentralized socio-economic network. To do this, perform the tasks in design, development, testing. Human chooses from a list of tasks most appropriate to his/her preferences / talent and begins the implementation of the plan, required to complete the task. During the work, the performer takes into account the time spent and additional costs.

After finishing the work, performer tells that he has finished the task and spent exact amount of time using some addition resources. Then experts of same specialization and at least the same level of qualification evaluate the solution . The purpose of their examination is to make certain in adequacy of the declared value of the work. In order to do this, they estimate three parameters:

  1. Reliability - how good and responsibly the work was carried out;
  2. fficiency - assessment of performance rate and resource costs;
  3. Functionality - the complexity of technology and additional features.

Next comes the stage of evaluation of community - ordinary users or customers who evaluate solutions in general: the convenience, aesthetic qualities, etc. When the proposal was liked by at least three unrelated users - it is deemed to be accepted and FreePenny is generated to the performer's wallet.

The total cost of the work in the FPN equivalent is formed according to the formula P = r * i * q * h + c, where r - the rating of decency, i - specialization index, of q - qualification of performer in this field of activity, h - the number of hours spent, c - the cost of necessary resources..
