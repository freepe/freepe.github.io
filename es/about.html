Misión Freepe

Freepe es un proyecto para crear una próspera asociación de personas libres en un mundo libre. La organización pública internacional sin ánimo de lucro "FreePe Foundation" ha sido establecida para implementar, apoyar, constituir y comisariar el proyecto. Los objetivos de la organización son: satisfacer y proteger los derechos o intereses sociales, económicos, ecológicos, culturales, creativos y otros de los individuos, empresas, instituciones financieras, académicas y autoridades gubernamentales; Contribuir a mejorar el clima económico, social y medioambiental en el mundo. Las principales actividades de la Fundación Freepe son::

  • Crear condiciones para el libre flujo de información,bienes y servicios en Internet usando instrumentos de pago alternativos mediante la creación de una red socio-económica descentralizada FreePe.
  • Proveer todo el apoyo posible, desarrollar el proyecto Freepe y todos sus componentes.
  • Crear las condiciones para que todo miembro de la comunidad sienta que tiene una participación plena y sepa que su éxito es el éxito de toda la comunidad.
  • Promover la unificación de los esfuerzos ciudadanos para crear una sociedad próspera.
  • Participar en la implementacin de programas internacionales, nacionales, regionales , locales y propios.
  • Colaborar con diferentes entidades públicas y privadas.
  • Facilitar la participación de miembros de la comunidad en eventos internacionales y seminarios relevantes para los fines fundacionales.
  • Contribuir a la organización de eventos educativos sin ánimo de lucro: congresos, conferencias , clases magistrales, seminarios,mesas redondas, ruedas de prensa, foros, talleres, convenciones, y otros eventos públicos enfocados en las necesidades e intereses de los miembros de la comunidad con la participación de expertos internacionales
  • Promover un estilo de vida sano, alimentación adecuadaPromoting a healthy way of life, correct nutrition, family and human values.
  • Acercamiento a los medios de comunicación pra conseguir los fines estatutarios facilitando la difusión de asuntos relacionados con los fines de la Fundación.
  • Facilitar la formación y educación para miembros de la comunidad y otras personas.
  • Developing of a full-fledged infrastructure to meet the social, economic, scientific, household and other needs and interests of organization members.

FreePe is a joint project

When one million people make together a common project, which includes one million unique ideas - it is much more effective than when each "pulls the blanket over himself," periodically reinventing the wheel. FreePe offers to unite and build a joint project that would satisfy the desires of each participant, allowing full modification of functionality and interface to suit personal needs, as well as helping to solve many questions in one place - from the management of personal finances and, ultimately, the whole infrastructure of the country.

Just imagine for a moment if, instead of a plurality of social networks, search engines, labor exchanges, blockchains - would be a unified versatile network, which includes the best ideas, projects, applications, components, and contains only high-quality products or services, and every user - is a real human. This would allow millions of people to communicate, work, plan, manage, process and resolve all personal and social problems through a universal interface that works on any hardware platform - computer, phone, car or robot. Now, just add here the fact that all this belongs to the users - that is, you!

The operating principle of FreePe

Participants of FreePe network set goals that are necessary for prosperity, which are broken down into tasks. For example, today's goal is to develop decentralized socio-economic network. To do this, perform the tasks in design, development, testing. Human chooses from a list of tasks most appropriate to his/her preferences / talent and begins the implementation of the plan, required to complete the task. During the work, the performer takes into account the time spent and additional costs.

After finishing the work, performer tells that he has finished the task and spent exact amount of time using some addition resources. Then experts of same specialization and at least the same level of qualification evaluate the solution . The purpose of their examination is to make certain in adequacy of the declared value of the work. In order to do this, they estimate three parameters:

  1. Reliability - how good and responsibly the work was carried out;
  2. fficiency - assessment of performance rate and resource costs;
  3. Functionality - the complexity of technology and additional features.

Next comes the stage of evaluation of community - ordinary users or customers who evaluate solutions in general: the convenience, aesthetic qualities, etc. When the proposal was liked by at least three unrelated users - it is deemed to be accepted and FreePenny is generated to the performer's wallet.

The total cost of the work in the FPN equivalent is formed according to the formula P = r * i * q * h + c, where r - the rating of decency, i - specialization index, of q - qualification of performer in this field of activity, h - the number of hours spent, c - the cost of necessary resources..
